Custom Home Kitchen and Bath Promo

CHALLENGE: Create a direct mail piece with a long shelf life to promote the Kitchen & Bath issue of Custom Home magazine

SOLUTION: I developed an informational brochure that was mailed along with a coaster series. I coordinated all of the production to get the coasters printed, die-cut and packaged with the brochure in a box with a custom label. All of the coaster designs were made from scratch in Illustrator.
