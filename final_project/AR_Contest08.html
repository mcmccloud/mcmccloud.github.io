ARCHITECT Contest Promo

CHALLENGE: Entice architect to enter a contest to win Bose noise canceling headphones

SOLUTION: I created a direct mail series consisting of a brochure and 3 postcards with the theme "Rise Above the Noise". All four pieces featured silhouettes of the tallest skyscrapers in select cities with stats on the buildings and the magazine ARCHITECT. The clean lines and bold graphics appeal to the architects' eye for design.
